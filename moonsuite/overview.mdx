## What is MoonSuite?

MoonSuite is an embeddable widget that enables users to buy Solana tokens with fiat currency (credit card, debit card, Apple Pay, Google Pay) directly from any website. Partners can integrate it via a simple iframe embed with full theming support.

## How It Works

The widget guides users through a complete checkout flow: enter the amount, choose a payment method, and sign in to receive tokens in their Moongate wallet or use their custom wallet.
